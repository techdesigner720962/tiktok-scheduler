## üöÄ Workflow Steps

### Step 1: Configure Your Account

- **Install MadHub** on your Android device, cloud phone, or emulator.
- Log in and add your TikTok accounts that you want to manage.

### Step 2: Setting Up the TikTok Account Warm-Up

1. **Access the TikTok Account Warm-Up Feature**:
   - Navigate to the automation section of MadHub and select **TikTok Account Warm-Up**.
  
2. **Configure Interaction Settings**:
   - Set the interaction probability (how often you'll engage with content).
   - Choose execution probability distribution to control the nature of your interactions (likes, comments).
  
3. **Choose Browsing Mode**:
   - Select between **Vertical Nurturing** (target specific content) or **Random Nurturing** (broad engagement across TikTok).

### Step 3: Schedule Your Posts

1. **Navigate to TikTok Auto-Post**:
   - Select the Auto-Post feature within MadHub's dashboard.

2. **Set Posting Schedule**:
   - Choose specific days and times for your posts to go live.
   - Configure the content of your posts, including captions and any media.

3. **Enable Loop Mode** (if needed):
   - Use this mode if you want to repeat the posting schedule for a specific period.

### Step 4: Monitor and Adjust

- Track your account engagement and adjust settings as necessary.
- Utilize the analytics to refine your posting strategy based on what works best.
